Our Drinks Menu

Welcome to our drinks menu! We offer a variety of beverages to satisfy your thirst. From refreshing juices to aromatic coffees, we have something for everyone.

Original Ethiopian Tea


Transport yourself to the heart of Ethiopia with a sip of the Abyssinian Leaf Brew. This enchanting tea, sourced from the lush highlands, offers a delicate balance of earthy richness and natural sweetness. The leaves are carefully handpicked, delivering a smooth, aromatic cup that is perfect for any time of day. Let the warmth of the brew wrap you in its embrace, offering a moment of pure relaxation


Ingredients: Ethiopian tea leaves, Hot water, Optional honey or lemon for flavor

Lionheart Bean Burst


Unleash the bold, intense flavors of the Roaring Rift Shot. This espresso is a true Ethiopian experience, made from beans grown in the rich volcanic soil of the Rift Valley. With its strong, dark flavor and a touch of natural sweetness, it provides an unforgettable, invigorating jolt that wakes up the senses. Perfect for those who love their coffee deep, dark, and full of personality.


Ingredients: Freshly ground Ethiopian espresso beans, Hot water (extracted in an espresso machine)

Silken Savannah Swirl


Indulge in the smooth luxury of the Silken Savannah Swirl. The richness of velvety steamed milk mingles effortlessly with a robust shot of espresso, creating a creamy, dreamy experience that’s as smooth as the savanna breeze. With every sip, you’ll feel a little closer to the golden plains of Ethiopia, where rich coffee culture meets comforting warmth.


Ingredients: Freshly brewed Ethiopian espresso, Steamed milk, A sprinkle of cinnamon or cocoa powder (optional)

Ethiopia Cold Rush


Beat the heat with the refreshing Frosted Jebena Chill. This icy blend of rich, dark coffee from Ethiopia's highlands is cooled to perfection, offering a cool burst of flavor. Served over ice and chilled to your liking, it’s the ultimate drink to quench your thirst while delivering that signature Ethiopian coffee kick. Perfectly balanced with a refreshing touch, it’s the ideal treat on a warm day.


Ingredients: Cold-brewed Ethiopian coffee, Ice cubes, Milk or cream (optional), Sweetener (optional)

Nile Root Glow


A vibrant fusion of nature’s finest, the Emerald Ruby Elixir combines the earthy flavor of matcha with the sweet, earthy undertones of beetroot. This radiant drink offers an unexpected yet delightful pairing that refreshes and nourishes with every sip. Whether you’re looking for a pick-me-up or a healthy treat, this bold blend delivers both in a deliciously unique way.


Ingredients: Matcha powder, Fresh beetroot juice, Almond or oat milk, Sweetener (optional)
