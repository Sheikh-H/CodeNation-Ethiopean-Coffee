Did You Know?

Coffee is believed to have originated in Ethiopia, specifically the Kaffa region. It is said to have been discovered by a goatherd who noticed his goats were unusually energetic after eating the cherries of a particular tree. From there, coffee spread to other parts of the world, including Arabia and Europe.
